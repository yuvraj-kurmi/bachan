Tribute to A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

A.P.j. Abdul kalam the "Missile Man of india" was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He served as the 11th president of india and was a renowned scientist, known for his work for his work in india's space and missile programs.

Achievements

  • Developed India's first satellite lauch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
